About

Built by people who actually do the work

Sean started Yardwise at 25 because he kept seeing the same gap: yards that looked fine on the surface but were being managed on guesswork — fixed watering schedules, no real maintenance plan, nobody checking the systems after install day.

He built a small crew around doing it differently — real design work up front, maintenance that's actually scheduled and tracked, and smart systems that get monitored after they go in, not just switched on and forgotten. Today Sean runs the crew alongside a handful of guys who handle the trucks, the install work, and the day-to-day maintenance routes across Sacramento.

Licensed and insured, built for the long haul — not a franchise, not a call center, just a team that shows up and does the work right.
