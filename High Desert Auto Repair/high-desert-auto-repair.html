What We Do

Full-Service Repair & Maintenance

From routine oil changes to complex diagnostics — most services available in-shop and many available right at your location.

Oil Changes & Fluid Services

Conventional, synthetic & full fluid flush services. Desert heat demands fresh oil.

Brake Repair & Replacement

Pads, rotors, calipers & brake fluid. Safety first — we'll give you an honest assessment.

Battery Testing & Replacement

Desert heat kills batteries fast. Free testing — replacement when you need it.

Engine Diagnostics

Check engine light on? Full diagnostic scan with a clear, jargon-free explanation.

AC & Heating Repair

Critical in the Southwest — recharge, repair leaks, replace compressors & more.

Tires: Rotation, Repair & Replacement

Tire rotation, flat repair, new sets & TPMS sensor service.

Alternator & Starter Repair

Electrical system diagnosis, alternator replacement, starter motor service.

Transmission Service

Fluid service, filter replacement, & diagnosis. Major work done in-shop.

Suspension & Steering

Shocks, struts, tie rods, alignment checks — especially important on desert roads.

Pre-Trip / Road-Trip Inspection

Heading out on a desert highway? Full multi-point inspection before you roll.

General Tune-Ups & Maintenance

Spark plugs, filters, belts, coolant — everything to keep your vehicle happy.

Our Story

The Mechanic Who Remembers Your Name

High Desert Auto Repair was founded on a simple idea: car trouble shouldn't mean a half-day spent in a waiting room under fluorescent lights.

Based in the red rock and open sky of the Southwest, we built our name on treating customers like neighbors, not invoices. Whether you roll into the shop or our tech rolls out to you, the goal is the same — honest diagnosis, fair pricing, and a fix that lasts.

We know desert conditions: heat, grit, altitude swings, and long drives between towns. We've built our expertise around keeping desert vehicles running reliably through all of it.

Honest every timeWe tell you what your vehicle needs — and what it doesn't. No upsells, no scare tactics.
