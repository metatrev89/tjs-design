Meet Jerry & Sons

Three electricians, one last name, zero franchise call centers. Here's the short version of how a dad and his two boys became the crew half of West Valley City calls when the lights go out.

Jerry Founder & master electrician

Started the business out of his garage with one van and a stubborn streak. Thirty-plus years later, he still answers his own phone and shows up to the tricky jobs himself.

Kai Electrician

Grew up handing Dad tools and never really stopped. Runs most of the new construction jobs and still can't say no to a good dad joke on the drive over.

Noah Electrician

The one who actually reads the appliance manuals. Handles most of the troubleshooting calls and takes a strange amount of pride in a perfectly labeled panel.

Mom Not technically on payroll. Still the boss.

She's never flipped a breaker in her life and has no plans to start. But every truck, every schedule, and every "are we sure about this quote" runs through her first. Official title: none. Actual authority: all of it.

Licensed master electrician Insured & certified
